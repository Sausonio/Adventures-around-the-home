Creator control

No automatic public posting

Direct Post is disabled. Adventures Around the Home does not automatically publish videos publicly.

TikTok Login authorizes the creator’s account. The Content Posting API uploads the creator-approved video as a TikTok draft. The creator then performs final review and publication inside TikTok.

You decide what gets published.Only use content you own or have appropriate permission to use. Review AI-assisted captions, narration, and edits before approving an upload.
