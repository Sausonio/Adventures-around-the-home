Frequently asked questions

How the creator workflow works

Clear answers about AI assistance, TikTok authorization, creator control, and where uploaded videos go.

What does Adventures Around the Home do?

It is an AI-assisted web application that helps creators organize media, edit short-form videos, review videos, prepare captions and narration, and upload creator-approved videos to TikTok as drafts.

How is TikTok Login used?

TikTok Login authorizes the creator’s TikTok account so the application can perform the creator-requested draft-upload workflow. The creator chooses whether to authorize the account.

Does the application post videos automatically?

No. Adventures Around the Home does not automatically publish videos publicly. A creator reviews and approves a video before it is uploaded, then completes final review and publication inside TikTok.

Where does an uploaded video appear?

The Content Posting API uploads the creator-approved video to the authorized TikTok account as a draft. Open TikTok to review the draft and decide whether to publish it.

Is Direct Post enabled?

No. Direct Post is disabled. The product uses draft upload, not automatic public posting.

What content may I use?

You must own the content or have all appropriate permissions needed to use it. This includes video, images, audio, music, narration, names, likenesses, and other submitted material.

What should I review before upload?

Review the video, captions, narration, facts, account destination, and upload details. AI-assisted material may contain errors, and the creator is responsible for deciding whether the result is ready.

How can I disconnect my TikTok account?

You may revoke the application’s authorization through TikTok. After access is revoked, a new TikTok authorization will be needed for future draft uploads. See the Privacy Policy for information about your choices.

How do I get help or ask a privacy question?

Visit Support for troubleshooting and contact instructions. You can also review the Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
